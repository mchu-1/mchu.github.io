Barcode Recording & Analytics

About

I am a graduate student at Caltech in the Elowitz lab. My research focuses on developing computational tools for analyzing and designing biological recording systems.

Research Interests

  • Machine learning for analyzing biological data
  • Molecular recording
  • Sequence analysis
  • Lineage tracing

Visualization of DNA barcode recordings as tapes using k-mer embeddings.

Software

  • Barcode Simulator - Simulate evolving DNA barcodes in clonal populations
  • BarcodeDenoiser - Statistical denoising of barcode data
  • Tree Builder - Reconstruct clonal lineage trees from sequential data
